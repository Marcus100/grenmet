# Actions during the hurricane or storm

These actions apply during active hurricane or storm conditions. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Action checklist

- All staff, except those required on duty in Meteorological, Security, and Electrical departments and one maintenance technician, should weather the storm at home.
- Manager of Meteorology or designated Officer in Charge must maintain contact with:
  - General Manager
  - Manager of Air Traffic Services
  - Director of Operations
  - National Disaster Management Agency / National Emergency Advisory Council
- All security officers should remain within the designated terminal building area.
- Employees required to work during tropical cyclone conditions will be assisted by the Authority with essential domestic affairs as necessary.
