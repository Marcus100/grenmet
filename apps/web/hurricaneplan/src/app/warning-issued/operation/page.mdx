# Warning issued actions: Operation

Operations coordination must be immediate and continuous across departments and stakeholders during warning phase. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Action checklist

- Liaise with Officer in Charge of CFR to secure CFR vehicles.
- Inform airlines, ground handlers, and concessionaires.
- Liaise with Manager of Meteorology.
- Liaise with Manager of Air Traffic Services.
- Liaise with Manager of Maintenance.
