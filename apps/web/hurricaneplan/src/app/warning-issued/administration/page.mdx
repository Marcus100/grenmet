# Warning issued actions: Administration

Administration teams should protect IT and communications functions needed for continuity during storm impact. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Information Technology Centre

- Run a full backup and secure backup drive in the safe.
- Disconnect and secure power supply units, computers, and printers.
- Ensure Meteorological Services telecommunications systems and internet links are operational.

## PABX operators

- Ensure all telephone lines are clear and available.
- Direct all tropical cyclone calls and enquiries to the National Disaster Management Agency.
