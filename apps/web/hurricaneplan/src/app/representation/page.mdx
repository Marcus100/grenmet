# Representation

The following positions are represented in the team responsible for managing the Emergency Operations Centre (EOC) during an emergency. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## EOC representation

- General Manager
- Director of Operations
- Manager of Meteorology
- Manager of Security
- Manager of Air Traffic Services
- Manager of Maintenance
- Manager of Human Resources
- Officer in Charge of CFR
- Duty Managers
- Quality Assurance and Compliance Manager
- Airline representative
- Refueling agent representative
- Officer in Charge (Lauriston)

## EOC coordination

The General Manager assumes the role of EOC Coordinator when it is imminent that a tropical cyclone will strike Grenada.
