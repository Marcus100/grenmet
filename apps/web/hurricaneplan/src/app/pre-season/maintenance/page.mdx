# Pre-season actions: Maintenance

Maintenance readiness covers electrical systems, generators and vehicles, plumbing, and structural checks for rapid storm preparation. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Electrical

- Ensure underground cable holes and trench systems leading to AGL building, terminal building, and central generating station (CGS) can be blocked at short notice.
- Ensure underground cable holes leading to navigation equipment (VOR/NDB) can be blocked.
- Secure a portable water pump.
- Ensure AC units and accessories are fastened and secured.

## Mechanical

- Ensure all portable generators are serviceable, including a generator for the Meteorological Office.
- Ensure all vehicles are serviceable.

## Plumbing

- Coordinate with Fields and Grounds to clean around the water tank if not already completed.
- Ensure all water valves are serviceable.
- Check that water truck is serviceable.
- Ensure lift pump for sewer lagoon is serviceable.

## Building and structures

- Check storm drains and downpipes.
- Ensure chainsaw is serviceable.
- Clear debris-prone areas on ramp and near facilities.
