# Pre-season actions: Administration

Administration readiness focuses on information systems protection and document preservation. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Information Technology

- Identify rooms where computers can be stored if equipment must be relocated.
- Ensure garbage bags are available to cover and protect equipment.

## Marketing and Properties

- Ensure damp-proof plastic sheeting is available to protect documents in the documentation center.
