# Pre-season actions: Air Traffic

The Manager of Air Traffic Services or designate should complete critical communications and equipment checks before season start. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Action checklist

- Coordinate with ECCAA technicians to ensure waterproof cover is available for ATC console and electronic equipment in the Technical Block, VOR, and NDB buildings.
- Liaise with ECCAA technicians to confirm all portable air/ground radios are operational.
