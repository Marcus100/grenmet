# Actions after the hurricane or storm

These actions support immediate welfare checks, damage assessment, restoration, and external notifications after the system has passed. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Source note

In the extracted source text, item 1 under this section appears blank or omitted. The list below starts from item 2 as presented.

## Action checklist

2. HR Manager should make every effort to contact staff members to verify wellbeing and immediate needs.

3. DANA team should conduct an initial assessment as soon as practical and report to the CEO and NEAC.

4. All rostered staff and available personnel should report to work to assist with restoration activities.

5. All managers should ensure their departments are fully operational in the shortest practical time.

6. Manager of Meteorology or designate should inform National Disaster Management Agency of storm status.

7. Marketing Manager should notify insurers of damage assessment and notify tenants of any damage to leaseholds.
