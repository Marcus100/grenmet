# Warning issued actions: Met Department

These are the actions required when a hurricane or storm watch/warning is issued. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Forecaster / Officer-in-Charge

- Inform the Manager of Meteorology immediately once storm/hurricane warning conditions are established.
- Inform the National Disaster Coordinator / National Disaster Management Agency immediately.
- Disseminate warning information to the National Disaster Management Agency / Emergency Operations Centre.
- Continue tracking the system and provide regular updates to relevant personnel.

## Manager of Meteorology

- Inform NEAC and the CEO after briefing by Forecaster / Officer-in-Charge.
- Secure at least one meteorologist / forecaster and two assistants to support increased duty demand.
- Provide weather briefings to EOC members at MBIA.
- Have staff prepared to work extended hours if needed.
- Make available foodstuff items identified for on-duty staff.
- Request assignment of a 4x4 pick-up van to the Meteorological Office.
