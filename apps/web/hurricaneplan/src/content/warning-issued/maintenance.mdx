# Warning issued actions: Maintenance

Maintenance teams should secure systems, utilities, structures, and vehicles for immediate storm protection and post-storm recovery. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Electrical

- Switch off all electrical mains and standby power generators.
- Install portable 11 KW generator as backup in baggage make-up area for Meteorological Office IT core room.
- Ensure adequate fuel, oil, power cord, and lighting for backup generator operations.
- Assign an electrician to the CGS.
- Cover all CGS equipment, including generator control equipment.

## Air Conditioning

- Ensure all AC units and accessories are fastened and secured.
- Switch off all AC units.

## Plumbing

- Check lift pump for sewer lagoon.
- Ensure Mobile 25 is filled with fuel and oil.
- Close all pipe mains.
- When hurricane watch is issued, coordinate with NAWASA to fill reserve water tanks.

## Mechanical

- Place all MBIA vehicles in inbound baggage area and ensure all are filled with fuel.

## Building and structures

- Secure terminal building, tower, and CGS windows and doors with plywood or metal sheeting as needed.
- Remove potted plants and unsecured terminal items to protected locations.
- Secure television monitors, including flight information display monitors, in the IT building.
