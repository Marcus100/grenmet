# Warning issued actions: Carriacou

The Manager of Air Traffic Services should liaise with the Officer in Charge of Carriacou to complete the following actions. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Action checklist

- Secure the air/ground radio.
- Take down and secure windsock.
- Secure the terminal building.
- Take down and secure VHF antenna.
