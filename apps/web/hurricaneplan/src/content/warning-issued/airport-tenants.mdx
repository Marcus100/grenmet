# Warning issued actions: Airport Tenants

All airport tenants are responsible for protection and security of property on their leaseholds. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Actions when watch is issued

- Survey leasehold and remove trash and loose materials.
- Place lightweight and movable equipment under shelter or secure appropriately.
- Inspect and secure permanent appurtenances such as doors, signs, windows, fences, and gates.
- Notify aircraft owners under tenant management to prepare for relocation to other areas.

## Actions when warning is issued

- Secure all remaining working equipment, including non-flyable aircraft.
- Secure or remove garbage cans, dumpsters, and signs.
- Do not place potential flying debris in airport dumpsters once warning is issued.
- Relocate flyable aircraft to other geographic areas, place under suitable shelter, or secure as appropriate.
- Protect breakables such as glass windows and doors; brace and tie down hangar doors as required.
- Cooperate with Airports Authority to complete all actions needed for collective protection.
