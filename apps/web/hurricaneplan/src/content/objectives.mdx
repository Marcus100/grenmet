# Objectives

This section states the intended outcomes of the Tropical Cyclone Emergency Plan and aligns departments around shared priorities. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Plan objectives

- Create a comprehensive, simplified, and direct plan of action for GAA in the event of a tropical cyclone threat or impact.
- Ensure each department fully understands its role and responsibilities to reduce duplication and streamline preparedness activities.
- Ensure effective communication between departments for successful emergency management.
- Ensure that the team identified to operate the Emergency Operations Centre (EOC) at MBIA is activated in the shortest possible time.

## Expected result

When executed as designed, these objectives support safer operations, faster coordination, and quicker restoration of airport services.
