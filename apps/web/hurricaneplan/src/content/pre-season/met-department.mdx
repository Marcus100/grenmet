# Pre-season actions: Met Department

The Manager of Meteorology or designate ensures staffing, supplies, and communication readiness before season start. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Consumables and equipment

- Ensure adequate stock of paper (8.5x11 and 8.5x14).
- Ensure printer, photocopier, and fax cartridges are available.
- Ensure flashlights, batteries, and first-aid supplies are available.

## Coordination and contact readiness

- Inform the Meteorological Office at Piarco (Trinidad) of work and home contact numbers for:
  - Manager of Meteorology
  - Assistant Manager of Meteorology
- Prepare and maintain phone/fax list for disaster preparedness officials and National Disaster Management Agency / National Emergency Advisory Council contacts.

## Staff preparation

- Conduct staff discussions on procedures during the hurricane season.
