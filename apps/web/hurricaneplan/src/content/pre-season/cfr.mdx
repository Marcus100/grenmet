# Pre-season actions: CFR

The Fire Chief or designate should ensure personnel support and emergency equipment are fully ready before the season begins. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## Action checklist

- Ensure the Fire Hall is equipped with first-aid kits.
- Ensure lanterns and flashlights are serviceable.
- Ensure all radios are working.
- Coordinate with the Stores supervisor to equip an ambulance first-aid kit.
