### Safe, secure spots

### 🅿️ Parking & Passenger Convenience

We aim to make your airport experience smooth and convenient.

Whether you’re dropping off or picking up passengers, parking while you travel, or accessing staff facilities, we offer a range of options to meet your needs.

Please familiarize yourself with our premises and parking guidelines before your arrival.

### 🅿️ GAA Public Car Park

The GAA Public Parking Lot is located northeast of the air terminal building, providing easy access to the terminal.

With a capacity of approximately 200 vehicles, this lot is ideal for travelers and visitors.

### ♿ Accessible Parking

For travelers with disabilities or reduced mobility, dedicated accessible parking stalls are available in the Public Car Park for your exclusive use.

### 👥 Staff Car Park

A designated Staff Car Park is available for GAA employees, tenants, and concessionaires.

This area is reserved exclusively for authorized personnel and provides convenient access to staff facilities and the terminal.

- Vehicles must not park in the designated pick-up and drop-off areas immediately outside the terminal building.
- Any vehicle parked in these restricted zones will be clamped at the owner’s expense.
