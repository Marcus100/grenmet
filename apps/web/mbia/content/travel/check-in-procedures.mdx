Timing is Everything—Plan Ahead

### Before you head to the airport:

### Confirm Reservation Details

Make sure to confirm reservations with your airline a few days before your trip, including both your flight details and baggage rules.

### Organize Your Required Travel Documents

Ensure that your passport and all required travel documents are valid and readily accessible before departure.

### Pack Smartly

Ensure your hand luggage contains only valuables and essential items you will need during your journey.

### Conduct Thorough Destination Research

Understand the local risks, health advisories, and cultural norms of your destination. Use official government websites and reputable sources to obtain current information.

### Share Your Itinerary

Keep a trusted contact informed about your travel plans, including accommodations, transport details, and excursions.

### Check-In Options

There are a variety of check-in options at the airport, including online check and self-service check-in. The options available to you will vary depending on which airline you are flying with. Please contact your airline directly for their check-in options.

Check-in via your airline's website or app Most of our airlines also offer the option to check-in to your flight via the airline’s website or their proprietary mobile app up to 24 hours before departure. Please visit your airline’s website to learn more about online check-in options and airline mobile apps

Check-in at your airline's kiosk Some of our airlines offer check-in at their automated kiosks. Experiences and options differ by airline, so please contact your airline directly with any questions or comments regarding kiosk check-in.

Check-in at your airline's counter prefer in-person help? All of our airlines offer in person check-in at their ticketing counter. Please visit your airline’s ticketing counter or visit their website for details on specific in person check-in polices.

### Check In Times

### Regional Flights

Check-in counters typically open 2 hours before the scheduled departure time. Arriving early helps ensure a smooth process, especially during peak travel periods.

### International Flights

Check-in counters generally open 3 hours before the scheduled departure time. Arriving early helps ensure a smooth process, especially during peak travel periods.

### Check-In Counter Closing Times

Closing times vary by airline. Always confirm with your airline for specific requirements, as some carriers may suggest additional time for check-in or have stricter cut-off policies.

### Tip

To avoid last-minute stress, plan to arrive at the airport well before the recommended opening time, particularly if you have baggage to check or special travel needs.
