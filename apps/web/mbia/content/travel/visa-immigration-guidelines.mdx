### Know before you go

### Why Choose MBIA?

Entry Requirements As is the case worldwide, airport security across the Caribbean has significantly increased since 2001. Consequently, all visitors to Grenada are required to present a valid passport and a valid return or onward ticket, regardless of nationality.

If you are planning to visit our islands, please note the following requirements:

- **Valid Passport:** Your passport must be valid for at least six (6) months beyond your arrival date in Grenada.
- **Return or Onward Ticket:** Proof of a return or onward journey is mandatory for entry of Non-Nationals.

We encourage all visitors to review these requirements carefully before traveling to ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience.

### Visa Requirements

### Immigration / Customs Declaration Forms

All passengers arriving in Grenada will be presented with an Immigration / Customs Declaration Form. This form will be provided to you onboard by your airline and there are copies available in the Arrivals Hall. It is advisable to fill out your declaration in advance of your arrival in order to expedite Immigration and Customs clearances. Passengers are also required to answer all questions on this form which includes the full address of where they will be staying while in Grenada. To learn more visit Grenada Customs & Excise Division.
