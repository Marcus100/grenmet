### For a relaxing start to your trip Start your journey in comfort and style

Make your waiting time fly by with a first-class experience before you board. Escape into one of our lounges—a quiet, comfortable space designed for both business and relaxation. Whether you need to unwind or stay productive, our lounges offer spacious, connected areas to work or relax and complementary pre-flight bites and refreshments to enhance your journey.

Premium Airport Experience

## GAA Executive Lounge

Relax, refresh, and unwind in comfort at the GAA Executive Lounge. Enjoy premium amenities, complimentary refreshments, business facilities, and personalized service designed to enhance your travel experience.

## VIP Diplomatic Lounge

The VIP Diplomatic Lounge provides a private and comfortable environment for government officials, accredited diplomats, and other eligible dignitaries. Managed by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs’ Protocol Office, the lounge offers a seamless and distinguished airport experience for official travellers.

Learn More About the VIP Lounge → Premium VIP Experience

## IAM Jet Centre Lounge

Experience luxury, privacy, and personalized service at the IAM Jet Centre Lounge. Enjoy premium amenities, private customs and immigration processing, elegant lounge facilities, and world-class hospitality designed for discerning travellers.
