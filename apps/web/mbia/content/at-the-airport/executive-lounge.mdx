### Sophistication for everyday travel.

The GAA Executive Lounge offers travelers a truly premium airport experience, regardless of airline or ticket class. This elegant, air-conditioned space provides a quiet retreat to relax, catch up on work, or enjoy light refreshments before departure. Guests can unwind in comfortable seating while savoring a selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages, all complemented by a scenic view of the apron. Designed for both business and leisure travelers, the Executive Lounge ensures your journey begins in comfort and style.

#### Services & Amenities

Enjoy a range of complimentary features when you visit the GAA Executive Lounge:

✈ **Beverages & Snacks** – Alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages, coffee, tea, wine, beer, plus sweet and savoury snacks. ✈ **Entertainment** – Large flat-panel television with cable channels, newspapers, and magazines. ✈ **Connectivity** – Complimentary Wi-Fi and internet access. ✈ **Business Essentials** – Local calls, fax, photocopying, and conference facilities. ✈ **Charging Stations** – Convenient charging outlets are available throughout the lounge.

### Conditions of Use

✈ Maximum stay is **three (3) hours** . Access may be restricted due to space constraints. ✈ Complimentary alcoholic beverages are limited to **two (2) drinks per person** . Additional drinks are subject to payment. ✈ Complimentary alcoholic beverages are limited to **local beer or wine only** . ✈ Children under **three (3) years** are admitted free when accompanied by an adult. All children must be accompanied by an adult at all times. ✈ **Smart casual attire** is required at all times.

### Methods of Payment

✈ **Credit Cards** – We accept VISA, MasterCard, and other major credit cards. ✈ **Lounge Programme Cards** – Priority Pass, Lounge Pair, and LoungeKey cards are accepted. ✈ **Cash** – Walk-in customers are welcome to pay in cash (**US$30.00 / XCD$80.00** for a three (3) hour stay).

### Fees

✈ **Executive Lounge Access Voucher** – US$30.00 / XCD$80.00 for a three (3) hour stay. ✈ **Annual Individual Membership** (One (1) person plus one (1) companion) – US$500.00 / XCD$1,350.00.

### Meeting Spaces

✈ The Executive Lounge can be rented for special events and groups at a one-off cost of **US$400.00 / XCD$1,080.00** for three (3) hours. ✈ Additional charges may apply for security, janitorial services, parking services, or other special requirements.
