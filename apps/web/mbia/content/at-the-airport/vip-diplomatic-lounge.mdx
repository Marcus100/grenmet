### For diplomats and government officials.

#### Government VIP Lounge

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs’ Protocol Office manages the Government VIP Lounge, which is reserved for diplomatic VIPs, including government officials and accredited diplomats.

Requests for access must be submitted in writing to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs prior to the date of arrival or departure. Approval will be granted only to individuals who meet the established eligibility criteria.
