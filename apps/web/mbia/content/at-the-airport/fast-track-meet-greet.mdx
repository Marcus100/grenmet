### Skip the queues, save time, and relax more time to shop, dine or relax before your flight

Skip the queues, save time, and relax with MBIA’s exclusive Fast Track / Meet & Greet Service. Designed to make your airport experience stress-free, this premium offering provides full escort through all airport formalities, priority processing, and personalized assistance for both arrivals and departures—regardless of airline or class of travel.

## Meet & Assist Services

### MBIA Departure Fast Track Service

On departure, guests are met curbside, assisted with check-in, expedited through Immigration and Security, and escorted to the Executive Lounge for a relaxing pre-flight experience. Fast Track allows passengers to move through airport formalities more quickly, giving them additional time to shop, dine, or relax before their flight.

### MBIA Arrival Meet & Greet Service

Upon arrival, guests are welcomed in the Immigration Hall with a personalized name sign, fast-tracked through Immigration and Customs, and escorted to their awaiting transportation. Our Meet & Greet service provides a smooth and stress-free airport experience with personalized assistance every step of the way.

Whether you’re visiting Grenada for the first time or are a seasoned traveller, our premium Meet & Assist service ensures seamless airport arrivals, departures, and connections for a comfortable and hassle-free journey.
