### BEFORE SECURITY

## Tambran by Tamara

Tambran by Tamara is a Grenada-based eco-jewellery and accessories brand founded in 2013 by artist-founder Tamara Prosper. Inspired by her love for art and with a commitment to sustainability, Tamaran turns everyday waste and natural materials into striking, one-of-a-kind pieces.

The brand transforms materials such as discarded plastic bottles, vinyl banners, scrap wood, and other recyclable waste — along with locally-sourced natural seeds and fibres like tamarind and donkey-eye seeds — into handcrafted jewelry and accessories.

The brand’s mission is more than just jewelry — it’s about transforming waste into value, celebrating local resources, and promoting sustainable fashion in the Caribbean and beyond.

### What makes Tambran by Tamara unique:

- All pieces are handcrafted and often one-of-a-kind; no two items are exactly the same.
- The brand showcases creative upcycling — what might once be considered waste becomes beautiful, wearable art.
- The aesthetic blends sustainability with luxury, offering eco-conscious customers an opportunity to wear statement pieces that reflect Caribbean creativity and environmental responsibility.
