### Engaging and supporting our local communities

At **Maurice Bishop International Airport (MBIA)** , we believe our success is deeply connected to the well-being and prosperity of the communities we serve. That’s why we actively invest in initiatives that create positive impacts, foster growth, and deliver lasting benefits for Grenada.

Our mission is simple: **to make Grenada the best place to live, visit, and work.**

### Our Key Initiatives

### Community Engagement Initiatives

The Grenada Airports Authority is committed to building strong community partnerships through health, education, culture, and outreach initiatives that positively impact the people of Grenada.

### GAA Health Fair

Held in 2023 and 2025, the GAA Health Fair promoted health and well-being within the airport community, reinforcing our commitment to a healthier environment and workforce.

### GAA Walk the Runway Fitness Challenge

Promoting health and wellness in the community. This signature event was held in 2024 and 2025, encouraging fitness and healthy lifestyles.

### GAA Career Day – Training Tomorrow’s Leaders

Inspiring the next generation of aviation professionals. Hosted in 2023 and 2024, this event provided students with insights into careers in aviation and airport operations.

### Arts & Culture Program – Our Artists Take Flight

Celebrating Grenada’s rich cultural heritage through temporary installations of contemporary art by local artists, including iconic works like *Spice Warrior* .

### Donation Boxes – Every Contribution Counts

Wondering what to do with leftover coins and banknotes from your holiday? Drop them in one of our charity donation boxes located inside the terminal. We accept all currencies, and every contribution supports local charities and meaningful community causes.

**Locations:**

- Landside – Near the Security Checkpoint
- Departure Hall

### Educational School Tours

Showcasing careers in aviation and airport operations while instilling pride in Grenada’s aviation sector through engaging and educational experiences for students.
