### BOARD OF DIRECTORS / BOARD MEMBERS

GAA’s Board of Directors is composed of seasoned business professionals whose diverse backgrounds and extensive experience provide strong governance and strategic direction for the Authority. Working closely with the Executive Management team, the Board plays a critical role in shaping the airport’s future and its impact on the island and the wider region.

The Board consists of ten directors with expertise across various sectors. They are responsible for setting policy and overseeing the general administration of the Authority’s affairs, ensuring operational excellence and long-term growth.

### MANAGEMENT TEAM/ EXECUTIVE MANAGERS

Our leadership team is committed to taking MBIA to new heights every day. At the heart of our success is a group of dedicated professionals whose knowledge, passion, and commitment ensure that every guest enjoys a safe and seamless experience.

The Management Team consists of department managers responsible for the day-to-day operations of the Authority. This team is led by the General Manager/Chief Executive Officer, who is accountable to the Board of Directors for implementing policies and driving strategic goals.
