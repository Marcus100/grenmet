### MBIA Runway and Road Upgrade & Rehabilitation Project (2020–2025)

Our Planet. Our Responsibility Building a better future Soaring sustainably Working toward a greener future Our world. Our Future Invest in a greener future Creating a sustainable future

On **May 19, 2025** , the Runway and Road Upgrade & Rehabilitation Project Completion and Handover Ceremony marked the successful conclusion of the Maurice Bishop International Airport (MBIA) upgrade project—an initiative launched in February 2021.

Executed by **China Harbour Engineering Company (CHEC)** , this milestone represents a major advancement in Grenada’s aviation infrastructure, positioning MBIA as a modern, future-ready gateway to the world.

### Key accomplishments included:

### Runway Expansion & Resurfacing

Increased durability and operational capacity.

### Solar-Powered Lighting System

Enhancing safety while promoting sustainability.

### Two Passenger Boarding Bridges

Improving convenience and accessibility for travelers.

### Apron & Service Lane Expansion

Supporting higher aircraft traffic and operations.

### Terminal Renovations & Canopy Expansion

Delivering greater comfort and shelter for passengers.

### New Emergency Operations Centre (EOC) & Administrative Headquarters

Strengthening disaster preparedness and operational efficiency.

### Advanced Air Traffic Management System & PAPI Lights

Ensuring precision and compliance with international aviation standards.

### Resurfaced Airport Bypass Road

Streamlining traffic flow and improving connectivity.

### A World-Class Aviation Hub

This comprehensive upgrade not only enhances safety and efficiency but also positions MBIA as a world-class aviation hub, ready to meet international standards and support Grenada’s economic growth.
