### Advertise With Us

At MBIA, our top priority is expanding and enhancing air service offerings to meet the growing demand of our vibrant population and thriving tourism sector. We are committed to creating new opportunities for airlines to connect Grenada with the world.

## Indoor Advertising Opportunities

Indoor advertising at MBIA is proudly managed by RG Media, offering tailored solutions designed to maximize brand visibility. From strategic placements to customized campaigns, RG Media helps businesses connect with travelers and elevate their presence within Grenada’s busiest gateways.

## Pop-Up Booths & Promotional Counters

Take advantage of unique opportunities to showcase your brand at Maurice Bishop International Airport through pop-up booths or promotional counters. Whether for retail sales or brand awareness campaigns, these high-visibility spaces connect you directly with travelers.

To learn more about how your business can benefit from an airport pop-up, contact the BDCC Department.
