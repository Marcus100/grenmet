Looking for a unique and convenient venue for your next conference, meeting, or special event? The Grenada Airports Authority (GAA) offers flexible spaces designed to meet your needs—whether you’re planning an intimate business meeting or a large-scale event.

### Available Spaces

#### Indoor Venues

✈ Conference Room – Ideal for corporate meetings, workshops, and trainings ✈ Training Room – Perfect for educational sessions and team development ✈ Waving Art Gallery ✈ GAA Executive Lounge – A premium setting for high-level business discussions

#### Outdoor Venues

✈ Historical Monuments – US Monument & Cuban Monuments ✈ Open Grass Areas – Perfect for ceremonies, cultural events, and community gatherings

### Rates & Charges

### Venue Rental Rates

#### Full Classroom

✈ Daily Rate: XCD $747.50 ✈ Hourly Rate: XCD $201.25

#### Conference Room

✈ Daily Rate: XCD $345.00 ✈ Hourly Rate: XCD $115.00

#### Waving Art Gallery

✈ Daily Rate: XCD $575.00 ✈ Hourly Rate: XCD $115.00

#### GAA Executive Lounge

The GAA Executive Lounge can be rented for special events/groups at a one-off cost of **US$400.00 / XCD$1,080.00** for three (3) hours. An additional cost is applied for any additional security, janitorial, parking service, etc. required.

### How To Book

### How to Book

Approval is required for all events held on airport property. To book a conference room or outdoor space:

✈ Provide detailed information about your event ✈ Note that fees and charges may apply depending on the nature of the event ✈ Contact our Human Resources Department via email or phone

**Please note:** All bookings are subject to availability.
