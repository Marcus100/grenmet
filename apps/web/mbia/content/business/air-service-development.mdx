### Let’s Build Your Next Route Here See the Potential. Come Fly with Us.

At MBIA, our top priority is expanding and enhancing air service offerings to meet the growing demand of our vibrant population and thriving tourism sector. We are committed to creating new opportunities for airlines to connect Grenada with the world.

### Why Choose MBIA?

- **Strategic Location:** Grenada provides an efficient gateway for airlines seeking to expand their networks across the Caribbean and beyond.
- **Growing Market:** The island’s expanding population and thriving tourism sector continue to drive strong demand for enhanced air connectivity and additional services.
- **Collaborative Partnerships:** We work closely with our airline partners to support successful route development, market growth, and long-term operational success.

### Join Our Network

Connect with a world of opportunity. Expand your reach, grow your customer base, and unlock new markets by partnering with MBIA.

## Opportunities for Airlines

The Grenada Airports Authority (GAA) welcomes carriers interested in introducing new scheduled or non-stop flights to destinations currently without direct service from Maurice Bishop International Airport (MBIA).

Whether you are considering seasonal, charter, or year-round operations, we are committed to supporting your success and helping you expand your network.
