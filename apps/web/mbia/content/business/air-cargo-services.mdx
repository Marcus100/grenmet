### Connecting people and products across continents

Maurice Bishop International Airport (MBIA) is home to a range of specialized companies dedicated to air cargo forwarding. Whether you’re shipping electrical goods, automotive parts, temperature-sensitive pharmaceuticals, or handling an urgent shipment that simply must fly, our trusted partners are ready to assist. Explore the companies listed below to find the right solution for your cargo needs.

## Cargo & Logistics Services

### Cargo (Throughput) Charges

A charge is imposed on throughput cargo at the following rates, payable by the owner/operator of the aircraft or his agents:

#### Imports

General Cargo – 25 cents per kilo

#### Exports

Fruits, vegetables, fish – 3 cents per kilo General Cargo – 10 cents per kilo

#### Exemptions

- Her Majesty’s Mail
- Goods in transit for the operation of an aircraft
