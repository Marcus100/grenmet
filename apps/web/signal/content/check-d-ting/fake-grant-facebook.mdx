## The claim

A Facebook page styled to look official is promising **$5,000 youth grants** to
anyone who messages, likes, and shares — then asks for a small **"processing
fee"** to release the money.

## What we know

No legitimate government grant asks you to pay a fee up front to receive funds.
The page is **newly created**, uses a copied logo, and routes everything through
private messages — all classic signs of a scam.

## How to spot it

- A **fee to receive money** is the single biggest red flag.
- Brand-new page, few followers, comments turned off.
- Pressure to act "today" and to share before applying.

## Bottom line

**Fake.** Do not pay, and do not share. Report the page to Facebook and warn
anyone who posted it.

## What to do

- Real opportunities are listed in **Opportunity Watch** with official links.
- If you sent money, report it to the police fraud line immediately.
