## The claim

A voice note and matching text are circulating on WhatsApp claiming NAWASA will
**shut off water across the entire island for three days** starting this week,
urging everyone to "store water now."

## What we know

NAWASA has published a notice for **scheduled maintenance** affecting **parts of
St. George only**, for a single morning — not the whole island, and not three
days. There is no official announcement matching the viral message.

## What is not confirmed

The "three-day island-wide" figure. It does not appear in any NAWASA notice, on
their official pages, or in any statement to media.

## Bottom line

**False.** Storing some water during the real, localised maintenance window is
sensible — but there is no island-wide three-day shutdown. Don't forward the
voice note.

## What to do

- Check NAWASA's official channels for the actual affected areas and times.
- Before sharing a "shutdown" warning, look for the official notice behind it.
