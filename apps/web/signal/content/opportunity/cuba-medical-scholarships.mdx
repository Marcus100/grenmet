## What's on offer

Fully-funded **medical scholarships to study in Cuba** for the upcoming academic
year, covering tuition and a monthly stipend. A set number of places are open to
Grenadian nationals.

## Who can apply

- Grenadian citizens, generally aged 18–25.
- Strong CSEC/CAPE passes including the sciences.
- Medically fit and willing to study in Spanish (language training is provided).

## Key dates

Applications close at the **end of this month**. Shortlisted candidates will be
called for interview in the following weeks.

## How to apply

1. Collect certified copies of your certificates and ID.
2. Get the application form from the Ministry of Education's scholarships unit.
3. Submit before the deadline — late applications are not accepted.

## Worth knowing

This is a competitive, multi-year commitment. If medicine isn't your path, keep
watching this section — nursing, teaching, and technical scholarships open at
different times of the year.
