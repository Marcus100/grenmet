## What's on offer

A grant scheme for **small tourism and hospitality businesses** — guesthouses,
tour operators, vendors, and creatives serving visitors. Awards are modest but
do not have to be repaid.

## Who can apply

Registered small businesses in the tourism sector, with **priority for Carriacou
and Petite Martinique** operators rebuilding after recent storm seasons.

## What it can fund

- Equipment and safety upgrades.
- Marketing and online booking tools.
- Training and certification.

## Key dates

Applications are open now and reviewed in rounds. Apply early — funds are
limited and allocated as strong applications come in.

## How to apply

1. Make sure your business registration is current.
2. Prepare a short plan: what you'll spend the grant on and the result you
   expect.
3. Submit through the Grenada Tourism Authority's business support unit.

## Watch out

No legitimate grant charges a fee to apply. If anyone asks you to pay to "unlock"
this grant, it's a scam — see Check D Ting.
